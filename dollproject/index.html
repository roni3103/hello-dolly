Meet RaggyMax..

A self taught 'cloth doll artist', Maxine adores working with colour and textile and escaping to her 'world of whimsy'. Inspiration for her raggy-dolls is drawn from her many passions and delights - fantasy art, boho and goth cultures. Some of her dolls are bright and bohemian, some may cross over to the dark side (but not too scary!!). Starting with the classic rag-doll, then adding a quirky twist.

Each one sprinkled with a little fairy dust...no two are the same, each one is absolutely unique and made to suit your own favourite colours and fancies.

Maxine is always so thrilled to send her dolls off to new homes - "When I sit back and look at a finished doll and smile, that is the best feeling". All her dolls carry a good spirit becauyse they are made with such love and care. Quite simply she loves what she creates, and hopes that you do too...
